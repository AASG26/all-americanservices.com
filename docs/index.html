Who We Are

All-American Services Group is a family of foundation repair and waterproofing companies guided by a simple vision: to be the preferred long-term partner for best-in-class business owners.

All-American Services Group was formed to support independent foundation repair and waterproofing company owners. Designed as a business-owner-friendly alternative to private equity, we do not buy out or take over businesses. Instead, we partner with owners, bringing like-minded companies together to unlock their full potential while allowing each owner to maintain autonomy over their operations.

Why All-American Services Group

All-American Services Group allows owners to realize value for part of their company while retaining brand-level ownership and operational autonomy. Our partners keep running their business their way while benefitting from being part of a larger organization.

All-American Services Group was built to support management teams. We help you deploy best-in-class technology and processes and handle the administrative burden of running your business so that you can focus on serving your customers, employees, and community.

How We Partner

We offer owners the best of both worlds: operational autonomy paired with the advantages of being part of a larger, well-capitalized organization made up of the industry’s top owner-operators.

  • Personalized partnership structures tailored to you
  • Maintain local brand and operational autonomy (“Secret Sauce”)
  • Support on administrative tasks, allowing you to focus on your customers and employees
  • Best-in-class technology, systems, and procedures to support continued growth

Our Strategy
